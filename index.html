About me

I love being a web developer and I have a huge interest in web application programming. I truly enjoy it because web technology is in constant growth and the development environment is a pretty exciting place to be in. Moreover, I can build something and see the result immediately. It is wonderful that I can show my application to thousands of people and share it with them. Programming extends my creativity and makes me passionate about developing. In my spare time I look for and buy non-working devices because I like to repair them and make them workable again. I think that if you want to be a good developer and improve devices or upgrade applications you have to know how they actually work. I find this hobby of mine very useful because it helps me to develop new technology based on an old one. The most important thing in developing is to lay the proper foundation and for me the easiest way to do it is to know well each part of the construction. What is more, fixing something that isn’t working brings me satisfaction and helps me to gain new experience.
